Chicken Avocado Burger

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 large ripe avocado
  • 1 chopped garlic clove
  • 1/3 cup Panko crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Add ingredients to a bowl and mix together. Shape mixutre into patties and grill.

Buffalo Chicken Wraps

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup diced avocado
  • 5 large soft leaves of lettuce

    • Toss chicken in buffalo sauce. Bake in oven at 375 degrees for 39 minutes. Cook quinoa as instructed on package. Mix together cooked quinoa, chicken, and avocado. Scoop into Lettuce bowls. Add ranch or bleu cheese for dipping.

Zucchini Fries

  • 3 zucchini sliced into 3" X 1/2" sticks
  • 1 large egg white
  • 2 tbsp romano cheese
  • 1/3 seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

    • In a bowl, beat egg whites and season. Place breadcrumbs, garlic powder, and cheese into ziplock bag and shake. Dip zucchini sticks into eggs then breadcrumb and cheese mixture and place on greased pan. Bake at 425 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Serve with marinara sauce
